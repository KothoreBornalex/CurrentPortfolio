    2024 - PC - FPS - Unreal Engine 5 - 1 Mounth - 8 Workers
    Gameplay Programmation - Feedback - Tooling
    Pitch
    Liberty Express is a narrative FPS where you follow the escape of Jack "the Revolver" aboard a train besieged on all sides by the Sheriff's police drones.
    Player Controller
    The player controller is designed to blend elements of the Old West with futuristic technology. It offers smooth, responsive movement with precision aiming, optimized for fast-paced gunplay.
    The controller handles classic Western-style gunfighting, including revolver, fast reload and hand combat
    Tooling
    To made it easier for the Game designers to work and adjust the game feel, I made the weapons, player stats, camera settings fully customizables.
    I used Primary Data Asset for it to be easy to use and efficient to iterate quickly.
