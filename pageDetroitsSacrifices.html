DETROIT'S SACRIFICES

    Detroit's Sacrifices
    2023 - PC - FPS/TPS - Unity - 6 Months - 1 Worker
    Gameplay - UI - LD & GD
    Pitch
    Detroit's Sacrifices is a student project made to learn how to code (from ground zero) and how to make a game on Unity. In this project I discovered what is a function, an array, an int... There isn't much logic in the story
    Player
    My player controller is a mix of old PS3 Hitman game and Payday 2

    On a side it let the player play stealthy with the crouch, the walk, the hacking, but on the other side it let the player brute force his way in with the assault rifle and run ability.

    Click To play the game: "Detroit's Sacrifices"
    AI And Boss Fight
    I made 2 AI, a typical soldier AI, Patrolling around or guarding places used in the stealth and actions part of the game.

    And a Boss like AI inspired from seikiro always chasing the player and trying to be as stylish as possible.
    Hacking In The Matrix
    After selecting the object to hack the player enter the matrix and then start a mini-game to achieve the hack, I didn't had the time to make multiple mini games but it was the idea
